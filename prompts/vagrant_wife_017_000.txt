The purpose of this scene is to reveal the character of Harry and to establish the conflict between him and his wife. Harry is revealed to be a jealous and possessive husband who is suspicious of his wife's relationship with Colonel Richardson. This sets up the conflict between the two characters which will be resolved later in the story.
