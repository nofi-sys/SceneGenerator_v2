1. Archie: impulsive young hero
2. Etheldene: saucy Queensland maiden, Archie's companion
3. the bull: older, stronger opponent in the fight; later, Archie's quarry
4. other bulls in the herd: watching and holloaing the fight

The purpose of this scene is to show the reader the character of Archie and Etheldene. Archie is impulsive and brave, while Etheldene is level-headed and resourceful.

- Two bulls fighting
- One bull is defeated and runs away
- The other bull chases after him
- Archie and Etheldene see this and follow
- Archie falls off his horse and the bull is about to attack him, but Etheldene saves him with her whip
- They all go back to camp
