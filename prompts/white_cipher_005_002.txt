A man purchases a stethoscope and a revolver in preparation for a meeting with a criminal. He is recognized by a Scotland Yard inspector, but manages to talk his way out of it.

-Fay: protagonist, a professional criminal
-Salesman: trying to sell Fay a stethoscope
-Inspector from Scotland Yard: old acquaintance of Fay's

The purpose of this scene is to introduce the character of Fay, a professional criminal. The author achieves this goal by showing Fay's resourcefulness and intelligence in outwitting the police.
