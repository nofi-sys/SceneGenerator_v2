The purpose of this scene is to establish the setting and to introduce the characters. The author also establishes the conflict in this scene.

- protagonist, a young man, unnamed 
- Aurora, the Grand Duchess, driving the car 
- the soldiers, wearing grey-green uniforms and covered helmets, led by a blonde giant officer

-Aurora and the narrator are driving at a high speed, and she tells him they are nearing the German border
-They are stopped by soldiers, who demand to see their papers
-Aurora tells the officer in charge that she will only show him the papers, and he agrees
-The officer is a large, blonde man, who is angry at being disturbed
-He looks at the papers and is satisfied, and they are allowed to go on their way
