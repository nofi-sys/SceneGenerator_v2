Maida finds a baby in a cave, and takes her back to the Little House. She leaves a note for the parents, saying they can claim their baby by proving property.

-Maida: protagonist, around 13 years old, daughter of a financier
-The baby: around 1 year old, brown-complexioned with dark hair and brown eyes
-Lucys clothes: Maida's younger sister's clothes, in tatters
-Betsy: Maida's friend, also around 13 years old

The purpose of this scene is to establish the conflict that Maida faces. She has found a baby in a cave, but she does not know what to do with it. Should she take it home with her, or should she leave it there in case the parents come back?
