The purpose of this scene is to create suspense and tension as the two boys are being chased by the bear. The author achieves this by having the bear slowly gain on the boys, and by having them lose their only weapon. This leaves the boys with no way to defend themselves if the bear catches them.

- Roy: level-headed, quick-thinking

- Nick Looker: the butt of a bear story
- Pop Burns: tells the bear story

Two boys are being chased by a bear. They manage to escape by climbing up a ledge that the bear cannot reach. As they are sitting on the ledge, they realize that they have no way to get down and that the bear is still trying to reach them.
