The purpose of this scene is to show the protagonist, Dave, using his ingenuity and resourcefulness to escape capture and defeat his enemies.

- Dave finds the workman repairing the television camera and kicks the small sectional ladder out from beneath the workman's feet, knocking him out
- Dave then dresses in the workman's clothes and finishes repairing the camera
- The pilot of the helicopter returns and Dave tells him that the workman is dead
- The pilot doesn't believe him and starts to approach, so Dave shoots him
- Dave then trusses up the pilot with friction tape

- Dave: protagonist, skilled in hand-to-hand combat
- The man with the rifle: enemy, looking for Dave
- The workman: enemy, repairing television camera
- The pilot: enemy, flying the helicopter
