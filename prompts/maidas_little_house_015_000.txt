-Arthur Duncan: protagonist, a boy from the Little House who is following Silva
-Silva: a girl who is running from Arthur and is apparently very afraid of him

Arthur follows Silva through the woods to the Moraine, where she suddenly turns and runs away from him. He calls after her, but she does not answer. Arthur wonders what could be the matter.

The purpose of this scene is to create suspense and mystery. The author achieves this by having the protagonist, Arthur, follow Silva through the woods at night. Silva is acting strange and Arthur doesn't know why. This makes the reader wonder what is going on and what will happen next.
