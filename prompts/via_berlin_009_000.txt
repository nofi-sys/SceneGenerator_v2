Herrman Ferreira, the brother of the Countess Elsa, meets Topham in the Thiergarten and they talk. Topham is preoccupied with thoughts of the Countess and is surprised to find that he knows none of the people around him. The fates are propitious, however, and he soon meets Herrman.

-Topham: twenty-five, clean-shaven, Gibsonesque, with the erect carriage that bespeaks military training
-Rutile: navy officer
-Countess Elsa: Brazilian countess who wants to become a German duchess
-Herrman Ferreira: brother of Countess Elsa

The purpose of this scene is to establish the setting and to introduce the character of Topham. The author achieves this by describing the scene in detail and by showing Topham's thoughts and feelings.
