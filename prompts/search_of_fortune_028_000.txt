The literary style of this scene is very descriptive. The author goes into great detail about the setting and the characters. There is also a lot of dialogue between the characters.

- The scene is set on a farm in the Bush. 
- The three friends are working to set up the new farm. 
- Archie is the best Bushman of the three, and he is thankful for his education and training. 
- The men speak of him as the young Boss.

The purpose of this scene is to establish the character of Archie as a hardworking and competent supervisor. The author also wants to show how Archie's background has prepared him for this role.
