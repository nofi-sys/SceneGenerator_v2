1. protagonist - a young man who is infatuated with a ghost
2. Baptiste - the protagonist's servant; shows discretion in his behavior
3. the dowager - does not see well; questions the protagonist
4. Madame d'Ionis - the dowager's daughter; scrutinizes the protagonist

The narrator has a vision of a ghost, and is so convinced by it that he starts to believe he is insane. He is happy and delirious, but when Madame d'Ionis questions him, he refuses to tell her anything.

The purpose of this scene is to establish the protagonist's mental state and to foreshadow the events to come. The author achieves this by having the protagonist act erratically and by having the other characters express concern for his mental health.
