-Teddy: the protagonist of the story, a young boy who is on a journey with his brother Roy
-Roy: Teddy's brother, who gets swept away by the current
-Bug Eye: one of the two wanderers that Teddy and Pop find
-Pop: the other wanderer that Teddy and Pop find

-Teddy is swept away by the current and ends up on shore
-He is joined by Pop and Bug Eye, who tell him they have not seen Roy
-Teddy is determined to find Roy before daylight
-The three of them search along the shore

The purpose of this scene is to create suspense and tension as the characters search for Roy. The author achieves this by making the search difficult, with the darkness and the river's tricks.
