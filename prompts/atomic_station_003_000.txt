The purpose of this scene is to introduce the reader to the world that Sheldon has landed in and to establish the character of the girl he meets.

- Girl is last of her kind, living with zombie-like mutants
- Sheldon is from the past and is shocked by what he sees
- Girl is excited to meet Sheldon and tells him there is a message from his past

1. The girl - She is the last of her kind, a human mutant. She is educated and has a scientific temperament. She is compassionate and caring, and feels a certain tenderness for the infantile mutants she looks after.
2. Sheldon - He is a scientist and has a scientific temperament. He is curious and inquisitive, and wants to understand everything. He is also compassionate and caring.
