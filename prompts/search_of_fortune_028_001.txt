- Archie is a strict but kind-hearted man who is able to put himself in the servant's place.
- The house is built of wood and is on high ground with trees around it.
- The house has a wing for the kitchen and sitting room and the other side has the best rooms.
- There is a storehouse that is full of supplies and a desk for the account books.

The style of this scene is very descriptive. It is written in third person point of view, and the tone is neutral.

The purpose of this scene is to introduce the reader to the main character, Archie, and to the setting of the story. The author achieves this by describing the homestead where Archie lives, as well as the store where he works.
