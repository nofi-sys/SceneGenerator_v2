The purpose of this scene is to introduce the idea of the lost city of the Quetzals, and to hint at the possibility that it is full of treasure. The author achieves this by having the man in the scene tell Hi about the history of the city and the legend of the Temple of Gold.

-Hi: protagonist, interested in the temple
-The man: knows about the temple and wants Hi's help to find the treasure
-Dudley Wigmore: prospector who discovered the temple and was murdered because of it
-The Spaniards: never found the temple
-The Quetzals: ancient race who built the temple; all wiped out by forest fever
