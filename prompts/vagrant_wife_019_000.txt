The literary style of this scene is light and playful. The tone is banterous and there is a bit of an edge to the dialogue as the characters tease each other. The point of view is third person limited, focusing on the conversation between Annie and Aubrey.

The purpose of this scene is to establish the characters of Annie and Aubrey and to set up the conflict between them. The author achieves this by having them argue with each other and by revealing their different attitudes towards love and relationships.
