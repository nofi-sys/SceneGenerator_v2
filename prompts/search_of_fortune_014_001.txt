The purpose of this scene is to create suspense and tension as the characters try to save the farm from a fire. The author also uses this scene to introduce the character of Archie, who will become an important figure later in the story.

- A fire breaks out on a farmstead in the middle of the night.
- The farmhands scramble to save the animals, but the smoke is too thick and they are only able to save the horses.
- One of the farmhands, Archie, is trapped in the stable with the horses.
- His fellow farmhands manage to break down the door and rescue him.

The literary style of this scene is very dramatic. It is written in third person point of view, and the tone is one of suspense and excitement.
