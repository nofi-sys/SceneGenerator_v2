Rosa's mother, Donna Emilia, has received a strange message from Seora Artigas. Her son, Estifanio, has disappeared. Rosa and Carlotta discuss the situation and speculate on Estifanio's whereabouts. Hi Ridden joins the conversation and tells a story about a schoolmate who disappeared and was later found. They all agree that Estifanio will probably turn up in the same way. Carlotta changes the subject to engines, which leads to a discussion about Hi's hobby of building small engines.

The purpose of this scene is to establish the characters of Rosa and Carlotta, and to establish the setting. The author achieves this by having the characters discuss their interests and by having them talk about Estifanio's disappearance.

The style of this scene is conversational and intimate. The point of view is third person limited, and the tone is warm and friendly.
